Pollo pizza is an Italian-style pizza featuring chicken ("pollo") as the primary topping, typically combined with tomato sauce, mozzarella, and ingredients like onions, mushrooms, or spinach. It is often regarded as a healthier, protein-packed option, sometimes utilizing grilled chicken, artichokes, or creamy feta. Key Variations and Information: Classic Components: Tender grilled chicken, creamy mozzarella, savory tomato sauce, and often onions, peppers, or mushrooms. Flavor Profiles: Common variations include BBQ chicken (with red onions and mozzarella), creamy white sauce, or pesto bases. Ingredients & Toppings: Popular additions include artichokes, roasted red peppers, spinach, feta, and provolone. "Pollo" Definition: "Pollo" is Italian for chicken, and this pizza is a staple in many pizzeria menus, including Pizza Nova. Chicken Crust (Crosta di Pollo): A low-carb, gluten-free variation where the pizza base itself is made from ground chicken, eggs, and parmesan cheese. Popularity: Known for being a, often healthier, lighter option compared to sausage or pepperoni pizzas. Nutritional Benefit: High in protein. Commonly available from brands like Dr. Oetker Ristorante, which includes marinated chicken breast, mozzarella, and spinach.
